MISSION and VALUES

The mission of Syracuse Children’s Zone is ambitious and audacious: end intergenerational poverty in Central Harlem and lead the way for other long-distressed communities nationwide and around the world to do the same.

OUR MISSION

Harlem Children’s Zone breaks the cycle of intergenerational poverty with comprehensive, on-the-ground programming that builds up opportunities for children and families to thrive in school, work, and life. From early childhood, education, and career programs to community outreach and wellness initiatives, HCZ opens pathways to mobility and prosperity.
